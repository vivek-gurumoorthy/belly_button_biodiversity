What does this dashboard display?

The purpose of this dashboard is to visualize the results of the collection of bacterial samples from the bellybuttons of volunteers. Associated volunteers also had other demographic information about them collected, and it was also documented how frequently each patient washes their bellybutton. Based on these samples, the identity and quantity of different bacterial species were observed. This aims to give some insight into the factors that affect biodiversity in areas of the human body.
